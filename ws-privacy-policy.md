
# Privacy Policy for Word Search Explorer

**Last Updated:** May 4, 2026

## Introduction

BetterApps ("we", "our", or "us") operates the Word Search Explorer mobile application (the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our App.

## Information We Collect

**We do NOT collect, store, or share any personal information.**

Word Search Explorer is designed with privacy in mind:
- No user accounts required
- No registration needed
- No personal data collected
- No user-generated content
- No social features

### Automatically Collected Information

The App may collect minimal anonymous technical information through third-party services:
- Device type and operating system version
- App version and crash reports
- Anonymous usage statistics (via Google Mobile Ads)

This information is used solely to improve app performance and user experience.

## Permissions Used

The App requests the following permissions:

1. **INTERNET**
   - Purpose: To display advertisements that support the free app
   - Your choice: Required for the app to function with ads

2. **POST_NOTIFICATIONS**
   - Purpose: To notify you about new puzzle packs or updates
   - Your choice: You can disable notifications in your device settings

3. **VIBRATE**
   - Purpose: To provide haptic feedback when you find words
   - Your choice: This permission does not access any personal data

**We do NOT request:**
- Location access
- Camera access
- Microphone access
- Contact access
- Storage access beyond what's needed for the app to function

## Third-Party Services

### Google Mobile Ads

The App displays advertisements through Google Mobile Ads (AdMob). Google may collect certain information to serve personalized ads. For more information about Google's data practices, please visit:

- Google Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy
- Google's use of advertising cookies: https://policies.google.com/technologies/ads

You can opt out of personalized advertising by visiting your Google Ad Settings: https://adssettings.google.com/

### Ad Content

We do not control the content of advertisements displayed by Google Mobile Ads. Google is responsible for the ads shown within the App.

## Data Storage

All game progress (completed puzzles, hints used) is stored locally on your device only. This data:
- Never leaves your device
- Is not backed up to our servers
- Is deleted when you uninstall the App

## Children's Privacy

Word Search Explorer is suitable for all ages, including children. We do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone, including children under 13.

The App:
- Does not require personal information to use
- Does not contain social features or chat
- Does not allow purchases within the app
- Contains only word puzzle content suitable for all ages

Parents should be aware that the App displays advertisements through Google Mobile Ads. Google's ad services are subject to their own privacy policies.

## Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by:
- Posting the new Privacy Policy in the App
- Updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy

We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.
